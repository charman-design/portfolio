About Me

Hello! I'm Maria Nalieth, a musical theater performing artist based in New York City.

As a junior at Pace University, I'm constantly pushing the boundaries of my creativity and performance skills. When I'm not in class or performing, you can find me at Popeyes or feeling emo in my room.

You can find me currently performing in my schools prodection of Jesus Christ Superstar. My recent rolls include Narrorator (Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dream Coat), Roger's Mom (Rent), Olive (25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee).
